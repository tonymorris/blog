Infix (_least to highest precedence_)

    
    
    |
    ^
    &
    < >
    = !
    :
    + -
    * / %
    ~
    



Postfix (_unary__)

    
    
    +
    -
    !
    ~
    



_Operators ending with colon (:) are right-associative._
